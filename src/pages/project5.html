Aura

Meditation & Mindfulness App

Overview

A mobile app concept designed to support meditation and mindfulness through a calm,intuitive, and visually soothing user experience.

This project explores the design of a meditation and wellness application focused on simplicity, clarity, and emotional tone. The goal was to create an interface that feels calm and approachable while helping users easily navigate different types of content such as guided meditations, sleep stories, and daily sessions.

The design uses soft color gradients, rounded components, and clean typography to establish a calm and welcoming atmosphere. Each screen is structured to reduce visual noise and guide the user naturally through the app’s features.

Special attention was given to the onboarding experience and content organization, ensuring that users can quickly access relevant sessions based on their needs. The layout balances functional elements with visual softness to support a relaxing user journey.
